Who?

Aside from being one of the most recognizable and greatest catchers in baseball, Yogi Berra is undeniably known for many of the things he said. Fondly referred to as yogi-isms, these quotes include well-known gems like "It ain't over 'til it's over." and "It's déjà vu all over again."

What?

Web developers & designers commonly have a need for fake text for their designs. Traditionally these placeholders were in Latin and started with the words "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet...". As such, text generators (Latin or not) are sometimes referred to as "ipsums".

Why?

There are lots of these text generators out there, each with its own set of twists on the same basic function — but how many tap into that rich trove of sometimes tautologous though mostly paradoxical phrases from the mind of Yogi? I for one figured we need at least one.
